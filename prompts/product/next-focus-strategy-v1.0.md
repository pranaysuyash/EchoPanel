# Next Focus Strategy (EchoPanel) — v1.0

**Goal**: Recommend the next product focus area with concrete deliverables and tradeoffs.

---

## Inputs
- Current stage: `<MVP | beta | GA>`
- Persona: `<who>`
- Constraints: `<privacy/offline/latency>`

---

## Output (required)
- 3 options (each with: impact, effort, risk, dependencies)
- Recommendation (one) with:
  - 1–2 week plan
  - ticket set outline (titles + acceptance criteria themes)

---

## Stop condition
Stop after recommendation.
