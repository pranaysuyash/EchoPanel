# Backlog Grooming (EchoPanel) — v1.0

**Goal**: Prioritize tickets by user impact, trust risk, and shipping leverage.

---

## Inputs
- Time horizon: `<today | this week | this month>`
- Primary persona: `<who>`

---

## Required discovery (if repo access)
```bash
sed -n '1,400p' docs/WORKLOG_TICKETS.md
```

---

## Output (required)
- Top 10 prioritized tickets with rationale
- “Do next” list (3 items) with acceptance criteria recap
- Any tickets that should be split (with suggested split titles)

---

## Stop condition
Stop after prioritization (do not implement).
