## üìä **Data We Collect**

### **1. Audio Data**

**How we collect it:**

- Text transcripts generated from your audio
- Speaker identification and diarization data

- Entity recognition results (people, organizations, topics)

**How we store it:**
- Locally on your Mac in `~/Library/Application Support/com.echopanel.app/sessions/`
- Under your control with configurable retention periods
- Never transmitted without your explicit consent

**What we collect:**
- App performance metrics (transcription speed, accuracy)

- Error reports and crash data (no personal content)

**How we use it:**
- Improve app performance and reliability
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Enhance user experience

### **4. Optional Cloud Services**
**Cloud transcription (if enabled):**
- Audio may be sent to third-party transcription services
- Based on your backend selection (OpenAI, HuggingFace, etc.)
- Governed by those services' privacy policies

**AI analysis features (if enabled):**
- Text may be sent to AI services for analysis
- For entity recognition, summarization, and insights
- Under your control and with clear indicators
