# Marketing (v0.1)

## Positioning
EchoPanel is a macOS menu bar app that turns any system audio into live meeting notes and action items without integrations.

## Core messages
- Live transcript from any Mac audio
- Actions, Decisions, Risks, and Entities in real time
- One click to listen, clear permission prompts
- Export Markdown or JSON instantly

## Audience
- Founders, PMs, recruiters, agency owners
- People running multiple meetings per day

## Proof points
- Works with Meet, Zoom, Teams, and in-person audio
- No calendar or bot integrations required
- Visible listening indicator and audio quality status

- Product Hunt and Indie Hackers
