Privacy and common questions

Short answers to what most teams ask before trying EchoPanel.

When does EchoPanel listen?

Only during sessions you start from the menu bar.

Do I need bots in every meeting?

No. EchoPanel runs on your Mac and works with the audio already playing.

How do I share notes?

Use Copy Markdown or Export JSON as soon as the meeting ends.
